# Credits — Fableborn

Fableborn is built with free, openly-licensed assets. Huge thanks to the creators below.

## 3D art

**Quaternius** — https://quaternius.com · https://poly.pizza/u/Quaternius
License: **CC0 1.0** (public domain).
Used throughout: characters & creatures, the nature/foliage kits (trees, palms, cacti, cactus
flowers, rocks, plants), the medieval village kit (houses, inn, blacksmith, stable, gazebo, well,
market stalls, cart, barrels, crates, fences, benches, bonfire, hay, cauldron), the modular dungeon
kits, and the monster packs used in the Champions' Gauntlet and Act II (the Sunscar Reaches).
GLB conversions sourced via the community mirror at
https://github.com/trebeljahr/quaternius-showcase.

**KayKit** — Kay Lousberg · https://kaylousberg.com · https://kaykit.itch.io
License: **CC0 1.0** (public domain).
Adventurer & skeleton character models, animations, weapons and dungeon props.

**Kenney** — https://kenney.nl
License: **CC0 1.0** (public domain).
Modular castle/nature kit pieces and particle/UI bits.

## Engine

**three.js** (r128) — https://threejs.org — MIT License. The renderer, GLTFLoader, and
postprocessing passes are inlined so the game runs fully offline.

All of the above 3D-art licenses (CC0 1.0) place the works in the public domain and do not require
