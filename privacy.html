Fableborn — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Fableborn is a local-first game. It runs entirely on your device and works fully offline. Your game progress (saves) is stored locally on your device and is not sent anywhere unless you choose to sign in.

What we collect

If you do not sign in: we collect nothing. No account, no analytics, no tracking, no advertising. The game never transmits your data.

If you choose to sign in (optional) to sync your saves across your devices, we store:

  • An account identifier from Sign in with Apple (and, only if you allow it, your email address — you may choose to hide it via Apple's private relay).
  • Your game save data (character level, progress, inventory — the same data already stored on your device).

That is all. We do not collect your name, contacts, location, usage analytics, or any advertising identifiers, and we do not use third-party tracking.

How it is used

Your account identifier and save data are used solely to let you continue your game on another device. We never sell or share your data, and we do not use it for advertising or profiling.

Where it is stored

Sync data is stored in our database provider (Supabase) and is protected so that only your account can read or write your own saves.

Children

Fableborn is family-friendly. Signing in is optional and not required to play. If a child uses the app, account creation should be done by a parent or guardian. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children beyond the optional account identifier and game saves described above.

Deleting your data

Signing out removes your session from the device. To delete your synced saves and account data from the cloud, contact us and we will remove it promptly.
